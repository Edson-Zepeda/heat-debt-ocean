# Final Submission Plan

Do not submit this file as your final writing. Use it to prepare your own Bow Seat submission.

## Title Field

Heat Is a Debt the Ocean Has Been Paying

## Category

Interactive & Multimedia - browser-based website.

## Short Concept / Use Description

Write this in your own English. Aim for one clear paragraph.

Required content:

- Say that the piece is a scroll-based interactive website.
- Explain that the viewer begins in an inland heat scene, descends through ocean systems, witnesses protection and ecological stress, then performs a reciprocity gesture.
- Mention that the changing background, meters, motion, and final interaction represent how the ocean sustains, protects, and reaches its limits.
- Mention any personal edits you made: code, drawings, colors, sounds, photos, or local details.

Draft frame to rewrite:

`[Title] is an interactive website about my connection to the ocean through local heat and water stress. Viewers scroll from an inland room into underwater ecosystems, where breath, coral life, mangrove protection, heat absorption, bleaching, and a dry future appear as changing visual worlds. The final gesture asks the viewer to return something to the ocean before the page can breathe again. I used [your tools/process] to make the viewer feel that even people far from the coast depend on the ocean, and that protection must become reciprocity.`

## Reflection Plan: 100-300 Words In English

Bow Seat asks you to answer all of these. Keep the final response personal, direct, and honest.

1. Why did you choose this medium, and how have you used it before?
2. How did you come up with the idea?
3. What inspired the work and creative process?
4. What did you learn about how the ocean sustains, protects, and inspires us?
5. What is your message to viewers?

Suggested structure:

- 40-70 words: Your real local heat/water moment.
- 50-90 words: Why an interactive website made sense and what you personally built or changed.
- 50-90 words: What your research taught you about ocean heat, oxygen, protection, and limits.
- 30-60 words: Your message about reciprocity.

Personal detail slots to fill:

- My local crisis or memory: `[write your exact lived detail]`
- My medium experience: `[coding, design, drawing, photography, animation, etc.]`
- My creative process: `[research, sketches, code tests, screenshots, edits]`
- My message: `[one sentence in your own words]`
