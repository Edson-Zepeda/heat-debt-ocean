# Reflection Template

Bow Seat requires a 100-300 word reflection in English. Use this as a checklist and write the final answer in your own voice.

## Required Questions To Answer

1. Why did you choose this medium?
2. How have you used this medium before?
3. How did you come up with the idea?
4. What inspired your creative process?
5. What did you learn about "Your Story, Our Ocean: How Our Ocean Sustains, Protects, and Inspires Us"?
6. What is your message to viewers?

## Writing Frame

Paragraph 1:

- Start with the local heat/water moment you actually experienced.
- Explain why a website/interactive piece fits the idea better than a still image.

Paragraph 2:

- Describe your process: research, sketching, code experiments, color/motion choices, progress screenshots.
- Mention how you represented ocean protection as a changing system rather than a lecture.

Paragraph 3:

- State what you learned.
- End with the message: the ocean protects us, including inland communities, but reciprocity is necessary.
