# Authorship Checklist

Use this before submitting to Bow Seat.

- [ ] I personally edited the code, layout, motion, colors, and/or assets.
- [ ] I reviewed every visible sentence before submitting.
- [ ] Any outside facts, ideas, inspiration, public-domain media, or copyright-free media are cited.
- [ ] I captured a progress screenshot showing my own editing process.
- [ ] My reflection is 100-300 words, in English, and written by me.
- [ ] My short concept/use description is written by me.
- [ ] The browser link, screenshots, or supporting documents open correctly.
- [ ] An adult sponsor's contact information is ready for the submission form.

Recommended final check: explain the piece aloud in one minute. If the explanation sounds like your lived experience of heat and water, the work is closer to being defensible.
